DESCRIPTION

Here, we proudly display the diverse range of artworks and activities created by the talented students of UP-FA2-GHt12-07, their designated class section. Under the guidance of their esteemed teacher, Ms. Carolyn Naces. These students showcase their creative prowess primarily through their major subject, Visual Graphics Design. From stunning digital illustrations to captivating graphic designs, our gallery offers a glimpse into the imaginative world of these budding artists.
